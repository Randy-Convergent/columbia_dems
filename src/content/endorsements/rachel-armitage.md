Columbia County Democrats is proud to endorse Rachel Armitage for Oregon State Senate, District 16. Rachel grew up in a working-class family right here on the North Coast, and she knows what it means to be knocked down by outside forces working against you. She's running to fight for good jobs, affordable housing, and access to health care for every North Coast family — because the North Coast deserves a real champion in Salem.
