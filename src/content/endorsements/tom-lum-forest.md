Columbia County Democrats is proud to endorse Tom Lum Forest for Oregon House District 31. Tom is a software engineer and MIT graduate who lives rurally in the district and has deep family roots in Oregon dating back to 1880 — he's served on the Banks School District Budget Committee since 2012. He's running to better fund public schools, reform the corporate tax, and bring public transportation to rural parts of the state. As Tom puts it: "I'm here to listen to you and collaborate to get where we want to go from where we are, to do the most good for the most people."
